Philosophy & Biography

Where Rigour
Meets Inspiration

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire."

Clifford Morara is a dynamic Mathematics and Physics educator specialising in the British International Curriculum, currently delivering rigorous A-Level, IGCSE and GCSE instruction at Priority Schools, Nairobi. A B.Ed (Science) graduate of Kenyatta University, he combines deep subject mastery with modern, student-centred pedagogy.

His approach fuses inquiry-based learning, mastery teaching, and strategic educational technology — from GeoGebra and Desmos to PhET Simulations and Google Classroom — to make abstract concepts genuinely accessible. Clifford builds not just exam-ready students, but confident, critical thinkers equipped for a rapidly changing world.
